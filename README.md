# Restaurants-Analysis
This is a Mexican Restaurants Analysis Dashboard. It combines restaurant data and consumer demographic data to analyze customer preferences, restaurant distribution, budgets, drinking habits,
transportation methods, and cuisine demand versus restaurant supply. The objective is to understand customer behavior and identify business opportunities for restaurant owners.

Dashboard Overview
Total Consumers: 130
Total Restaurants: 138
Restaurants are spread across cities such as San Luis Potosi, Ciudad Victoria, Cuernavaca, and Jiutepec.
The dashboard also analyzes customer demographics like marital status, occupation, budget, children, drink level, and transport mode.
**Key Business Insights**
1. San Luis Potosi dominates the restaurant market

The city-wise restaurant count shows San Luis Potosi has 84 restaurants, significantly higher than Ciudad Victoria (23), Cuernavaca (21), and Jiutepec (2). This indicates that restaurant business activity is heavily concentrated in San Luis Potosi.

2. Most customers are students and single

Consumer demographics show:

86% are students
93% are single

This suggests the primary customer segment consists of young adults, so restaurants should focus on affordable pricing, casual dining, and student-friendly promotions.

3. Medium-budget restaurants have the largest customer base

Budget analysis indicates:

69% prefer medium-budget restaurants
Only 4% prefer high-budget restaurants

This means restaurants targeting the medium-price segment are likely to attract the largest number of customers.

4. Public transport is the most common travel mode

Transportation analysis shows:

62.6% use public transport
26.7% use cars
10.7% walk

This suggests restaurants located near public transportation hubs may receive higher customer traffic.

5. Demand-Supply Gap exists for certain cuisines

The Gap Analysis compares consumer demand with the number of restaurants offering specific cuisines. Some cuisines show a large gap between customer interest and restaurant availability, indicating potential opportunities for new restaurant businesses to enter those cuisine segments.
